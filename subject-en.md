# Managing Memberships and Projects for an Association

This internship project focuses on creating a program to manage memberships and projects undertaken by an association.

## Here are some key points:

- The association's members are: President, Vice-President, Treasurer, Assistant Treasurer, General Secretary, Assistant General Secretary, Advisors, and members.

- The association's board members are also members.

- To enable the association to carry out its tasks and small projects, each member pays an annual membership fee based on the results of the general assembly, against a receipt signed and stamped by the association.

- The association undertakes community projects (drilling wells, building and renovating mosques, widows' and orphans' homes, etc.).

- The sources of funding for these projects are: association members (their own money or donations), government donations, and donations from other associations.

- The funding source for some projects is subscriber contributions (each subscriber must pay a fee).

- Before starting each project, the association's board creates a committee of members who will manage the project from start to finish.

- On the platform, committee members must be able to enter transactions for each project according to their respective access rights.

- The committee for each project must enter the amount of each member's donation, the payment receipt, and the supplier invoices.

- At the end of each project, the committee must present its report to the association members, detailing income and expenses, along with invoices, vouchers, and copies of checks.

- The association's board members must present their annual report to the subscribers once a year.

- Every transaction made on the association's bank account must be documented, and a photo of the check or transfer order must be included with the reports.

- On the platform, each subscriber will be able to view their history, including their donations, subscription status, and outstanding balances owed to the association.
